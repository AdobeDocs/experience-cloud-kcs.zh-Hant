# 停止將Analytics Media（視訊）資料轉送至AAM

## 說明

如何防止Analytics媒體（視訊）資料轉送至AAM?

## 解析度


如果不想將其Analytics媒體/視訊追蹤資料轉送至Audience Manager，則可在媒體收集API實作中，將analytics.optOutServerSideForwarding設定設為true。 如需詳細資訊，請前往： [https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/media-analytics/using/metrics-and-metadata/audio-video-parameters.html?lang=en#opt-out-server-side-forwarding](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/docs/media-analytics/using/metrics-and-metadata/audio-video-parameters.html?lang=en#opt-out-server-side-forwarding)
